Programs


Our programs apply evidence-based practices in an inclusive small group setting to effectively meet the needs of each child. Appropriate developmental goals are established for both atypical and typically developing children with progress consistently monitored through ongoing assessments. 




Language Development Program

Our inclusive Language Development Program is designed to meet the communication milestones of toddlers, when speech and language development is most critical. Each child is given an initial assessment to determine receptive and expressive language skills. Individualized goals are used to address any deficit area. From children who require specialized instruction to those who simply need an opportunity to socialize, the Language Development Program addresses their needs in an inclusive small group setting.


Kindergarten Readiness Program

In recent years, school systems have begun to look at a child’s physical, social, and cognitive development to determine if he or she is ready for kindergarten. This new approach has made the need for quality pre-kindergarten programs much more prevalent. At The Sanctuary Pre-K, pre-readiness skills are incorporated into each student’s individualized learning goals. Assessments are conducted regularly to determine student progress and problem areas.
